Our Story

Started with
a single cutting

Terra Verde began in 2019 when founder Meera Nair propagated her first Pothos from a stolen office cutting. What started as a balcony obsession grew into Pune's most beloved urban plant shop.

We source plants from ethical nurseries across India, working with small family growers who share our passion for sustainable horticulture.

Plant Care Made Simple

Every plant tells you what it needs — we help you listen.

Watering

Most indoor plants need water only when the top inch of soil feels dry. Overwatering is the #1 plant killer.

Light

Know your light — bright indirect sun suits most tropical plants, while succulents crave direct sunlight.

Repotting

Repot when roots circle the drainage holes. Spring is the best time — plants are waking up and ready to grow.
