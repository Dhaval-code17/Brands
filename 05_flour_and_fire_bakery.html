Our Story

A wood-fired
obsession that
never stopped

Flour & Fire started in Rohan's home kitchen in 2018, baking loaves for neighbors on weekends. The waiting list grew faster than he could handle — so he built a bakery.

Today we bake 200+ loaves daily using the same levain starter Rohan has fed every morning since that first weekend.
