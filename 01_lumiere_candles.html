The art of
slow living

We believe fragrance is a ritual — a moment to pause, breathe, and return to yourself. Each Lumière candle is crafted to transform ordinary spaces into sanctuaries.

01

Trim your wick

Before each burn, trim to 5mm for a clean, even flame.

02

First burn

Allow the wax pool to reach all edges — 2 to 3 hours.

03

Breathe deeply

Let the fragrance fill your space and your senses.
